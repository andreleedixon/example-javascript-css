The Rain In The Midnight Blue

Imagine a world where there is no fear, pain, hunger, and devoid of all negativity. Place yourself at the feet of peace, nesting on the soft clouds of tranquility, while feasting at the table of fellowship in the comfort of love. We challenge ourselves to be free and we demand from others nothing more than the respect they expect from us. We find ourselves carving rules in society in the same way that we carve our lovers names in our favorite trees.

I try to remember the most important things my Father taught me:

  • Love
  • Respect
  • Generosity
  • Laughter
  • Fellowship

When people want me to list my top five things that make me come alive.

I want them to take with them these items:

  1. Love
  2. Family
  3. Friends
  4. Fellowship
  5. Joy

Always remember what is important to you and what makes you feel good about yourself!
